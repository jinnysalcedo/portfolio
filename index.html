About Me

My name is Jinthana Salcedo and I am a 25 year old college student at California State University, Hayward. I am a graphic design major and I am in my 3rd year of college as a transfer student from Los Medanos College. I love designing resumes, banners, logos, as well as doing photography on the side. I am currently working at AT&T Park in San Francisco, CA as a fan photographer for the San Francisco Giants. As a designer, I am here to listen to your needs and wants and to make sure you are leaving with more than what you expected.
